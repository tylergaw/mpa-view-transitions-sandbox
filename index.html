MPA View Transitions Sandbox

A collection of example view transitions for multi-page sites.

Full page transitions

  • Basic No CSS customization, browser defaults
  • Slide Left All pages slide in from the right to the left
  • Slide Left In and Out All pages slide in from the right to left and out to the left
  • Slide In/Out Unique to Page Each page slides in and out in a different direction
  • Scale Each page scales in and out
  • Blur Each page blurs in and out

Content transitions

  • Grid Item View A standard grid of items and a transition to and from a single item view
  • Sequenced Items A number of elements transition in a sequence. (pretty hacky still)
